A Vehicle for Your Lifestyle

The Switchblade is a three-wheel, street legal vehicle that you drive from your garage to a nearby airport. At the airport, the wings swing out and the tail extends in under three minutes. You then fly to your destination at up to 160 mph, land, and drive to where you want to go.

About Sam Bousfield and Samson Sky

Sam Bousfield is the CEO and designer of the Switchblade. A fourth-generation innovator from Nevada City, California, Sam earned his BS with Dean's Honors from Cal Poly SLO in 1979 and has spent over 13 years designing, prototyping, and preparing the Switchblade for production.

He holds 8+ patents on the Switchblade design, delivered a TEDx talk at Wake Forest University ("Flight to the Future"), and received the GSA Global Innovator Award 2025 at a Congressional ceremony on Capitol Hill.

Samson Sky is headquartered in Redmond, Oregon. The company's manufacturing breakthrough cuts carbon fiber production costs in half and enables parts to be made 8x faster than traditional methods.
